What We Do

Complete Tree Care,
Any Kine Tree

Whether it's a massive monkeypod threatening your roof or a coconut palm needing a trim — we've seen it all and handle it all. Professionally, safely, and with full insurance.

🌳

Tree Removal

Safe, efficient removal of any size tree — from residential yards to commercial properties. We handle the cleanup too, leaving your property spotless.

✂️

Tree Trimming & Pruning

Proper pruning promotes healthy growth, improves structure, and keeps branches away from your roof, power lines, and fences.

🌴

Palm Tree Services

Coconut, royal, and ornamental palms need specific care. We trim, clean, and remove palms — including skinning royal palms for that polished look.

⚡

Emergency Storm Response

Kauai storms don't wait. We're on call 24/7 to clear fallen trees, remove hazardous limbs, and restore safe access to your property.

🪵

Stump Grinding & Removal

Eliminate unsightly stumps and prevent regrowth. Our grinding equipment handles stumps of all sizes, leaving your yard smooth and usable.

🔬

Arborist Consulting

ISA-certified arborist reports for permits, real estate transactions, and health assessments. Expert advice on your trees' condition and long-term care.

🏡

Land Clearing

Preparing for construction or reclaiming overgrown land? We clear vegetation and trees efficiently, handling debris hauling and site prep.

💊

Tree Health & Treatment

Diagnose and treat disease, pest infestations, and nutrient deficiencies. We help save valuable trees before removal becomes necessary.

🧹

Debris Hauling & Chipping

Full cleanup after every job. We chip, haul, and dispose of all green waste — or leave wood chips on-site as mulch if you prefer.
